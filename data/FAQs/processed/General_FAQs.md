## [Others]
**Question:** What are the rewards and recognition programs at Axis Bank?

**Answer:**
We have a suite of recognition programs that honor excellence, tenure, and impact:
• Champions Awards – Celebrating individuals who consistently deliver outstanding results and embody Axis Bank’s values in action.
• Anchors – Recognizing employees who demonstrate exceptional performance by living the 4 mantras - Own the customer, Own the brand, Act with speed & Zero pendency.
• OGs – Honoring tenured employees who have reached significant milestones of 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, and 35 years at Axis Bank. This program celebrates their enduring commitment and legacy.
• Ahead of the Curve – Spotlighting those who lead with agility, innovation, and future-readiness, helping Axis Bank stay ahead in a dynamic environment.
• Squad Keepers – Applauding frontline managers with zero attrition in their teams who foster collaboration, inclusivity, and a strong sense of camaraderie to help retain the team.
• Retiring with Pride – A heartfelt celebration of our vintage employees who are retiring, honoring their journey, contributions, and the lasting impact they leave behind.
These programs are more than just awards—they are a reflection of our culture of appreciation, where every employee’s journey is celebrated and every contribution matters.

## [Others]
**Question:** What are the initiatives at Axis for DEI?

**Answer:**
Axis Bank’s Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI) strategy is built on creating an inclusive and equitable workplace. Key initiatives include:
• #HouseWorkIsWork: A program that recognizes the transferable skills of individuals returning from career breaks, especially homemakers.
• Gender Representation in Teams: A policy to ensure every team includes at least one woman.
• Axis GIG-A: A fully remote, work-from-anywhere model, widely adopted by freelancers and flexible workers.
• Pause for Bias: Internal programs aimed at addressing unconscious bias, supported by a formal D&I Council.
• AxisVIBE: A platform for collaborative DEI learning, hosting events and discussions with participation from various organizations.
• Mentorship Programs: Structured mentoring initiatives like Astros for managerial development, with a focus on ensuring gender diversity among mentees.

*Source: DEI Policy*
